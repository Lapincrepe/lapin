Fun Facts About Grumpy Cat

  1. She's a girl. Her constant look of disapproval might remind you of your own mother at times.
  2. She is a mixed breed. Her owners do not know her exact breed but believe she is mixed with Persian, Ragdoll or Snowshoe.
  3. She loves to cuddle! Regardless of her grumpy reputation, Tardar Sauce's owners say that she is a cuddly kitty who loves to be held and rubbed.
